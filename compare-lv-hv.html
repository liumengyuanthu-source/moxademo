Can you compare the specific differences between the EDS-4008-LV and the EDS-4008-HV models?

The key difference is the power-input range. Choose EDS-4008-LV for low-voltage DC environments. Choose EDS-4008-HV when the installation requires high-voltage DC or AC input. Their Ethernet port count and standard operating-temperature range are the same.

Engineering specification EDS-4008-LV EDS-4008-HV
Operating voltage 9.6 to 60 VDCDesigned for 12/24/48 VDC systems with redundant dual inputs. 88 to 300 VDCFor high-voltage DC installations.

Installation DIN-rail mounting; optional wall mounting. DIN-rail mounting; optional wall mounting.
Choose LV when Your control cabinet uses 12/24/48 VDC or another supply within 9.6 to 60 VDC.
Choose HV when Your site requires 88 to 300 VDC or 85 to 264 VAC input.
