Introduction

Futureproof Your Industrial Network Security

With over 35 years of expertise in industrial automation, Moxa is proud to be one of the first companies globally to achieve both IEC 62443-4-1 certification for our secure development life-cycle and IEC 62443-4-2 certification for a range of our networking products. Trust our secure switches, routers, firewalls, and network management software to help you build a resilient industrial network while reducing security risks and disruption to operations.

Key Value

At the Core of Our Secure Networking Solutions

Protect industrial devices and networks with secure devices, stronger authentication and authorization, and layered defenses that reduce potential OT threats.

Security is built into the product lifecycle through IEC 62443-4-1 processes, secure boot, signed firmware, vulnerability management, and hardened device configuration.

IEEE 802.1X, role-based access, port security, secure protocols, and auditable activity strengthen identity and access control across industrial networks.

Segmentation, firewall policies, intrusion prevention, encrypted tunnels, ACLs, and abnormal-activity alerts reduce the attack surface without interrupting operations.
