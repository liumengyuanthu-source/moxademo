Overview

Serial device connectivity for Ethernet networks

The NPort 5100 Series makes serial devices network-ready in an instant. These compact device servers support RS-232, RS-422, and RS-485 interfaces, 10/100BaseT(X) Ethernet, Real COM/TTY operation, web-based configuration, SNMP MIB-II management, and wide-temperature variants for demanding industrial sites.

Choose a model for RS-232, RS-422/485, or software-selectable RS-232/422/485 serial interfaces.

Real COM/TTY continuity

Keep existing PC software connected through Real COM and Real TTY modes while moving devices onto Ethernet.

Compact 1-port design

Fit serial-to-Ethernet conversion into tight cabinets, machine panels, access terminals, and remote equipment boxes.

Wide-temperature options
